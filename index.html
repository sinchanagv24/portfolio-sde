About Me

Hi! I’m Sinchana G V (Legal name: Sinchana Gupta Garla Venkatesha), a Software Engineer focused on building reliable backend services and full-stack web applications. I hold a Master’s in Data Science from the University of Maryland and have 2+ years of hands-on experience designing REST APIs, integrating legacy systems, and shipping production-grade features with Python/FastAPI, React, SQL, and Docker on AWS/Snowflake.

Previously, I worked at Mann+Hummel where I built real-time analytics dashboards and automated data pipelines into Snowflake to support operations. Before that, I was an Application Development Associate at Accenture, where I designed a frontend web application to retrieve vehicle VIN data from legacy mainframe services and generate Certificates of Conformity (COC). The solution bridged web UI ↔ REST endpoints ↔ COBOL/Natural/Adabas backends and significantly reduced manual issuance time.

I enjoy taking projects from prototype to production: clear interfaces, schema validation, observability, and CI for fast, safe iteration. I regularly build and share personal projects in this space, which I open-source on GitHub.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me playing table tennis, hiking, exploring new places, or trying out unique coffee spots.

I’m currently seeking full-time Software Engineer roles (Backend / Full-Stack / Platform). If my background aligns with your team, I’d love to connect.
